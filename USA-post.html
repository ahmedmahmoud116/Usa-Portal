Welcome to the USA-PORTAL


We are group of students who are working hard to make this portal so that we can help other students

What is our purpose?

We are using cloud storages to store all the study data of University to help students get it anytime anywhere without any hustle

Why are we doing it?

We are students ourselves and we felt that students can lose or not get study data at all due to various reasons,
and University lacked an online library so we stood up for the cause

What do we get or earn from it?

Well we earn no revenue or profit from this portal, this portal is totally dedicated to students
